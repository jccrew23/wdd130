Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to reach a wider audience to build interest in recreationally white water rafting with "company".

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our Target audience can be defined as middle to high income middle-aged adults between the ages of 35-55 visiting the Wyoming area. They are often childless or have older children and enjoy exploring the outdoors. They will often find joy in adrenaline-seeking sports such as ziplining or skydiving. They will most likely take enjoyment in water sports.

What questions will we answer for them on the website? They may be asking "Where can I find a rental raft or a rafting guide?", "How much does white water rafting cost" or "How can I best explore all that Wyoming outdoors have to offer?".

How will the target customers access my site? Most likely they will view it on a mobile device probably an apple product.
