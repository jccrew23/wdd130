Come explore the beautiful canyons and valleys! Whether you are a seasoned white water rafter or a "greenie" we've got a trip for YOU! Family fun, Mild White Water and WILD White water; these trips are for YOU, and we want to make sure you have the adventure of a lifetime, no matter your age or experience! Are you looking to meander through nature and catch the magnificent sights? Whether it’s a 4 hour experience or an 3 day journey, there is a river waiting for you.

WILD-Calling all thrill-seekers! Our challenging rafting trips are for those who love a good heart-pounding adventure and are not suitable for kids. The minimum age is 17.
Mild-Moderate rafting trips are exciting, splashy and our most popular. The rapids are the perfect intro to whitewater with a healthy mix of paddling and time to enjoy the stunning scenery.
Family Fun-Fully immerse yourself in the beautiful wilderness with a unique view from the river with these family-friendly rafting trips. Mild trips feature smaller rapids and the minimum age ranges from 3 to 6 depending on the trip.

Come experience a premier white water rafting adventure! The South, Middle and North Forks of the American River offer a wide variety of rafting options for all ages and skill levels. We have something for everyone including large groups! As one of the first California rafting companies to offer a special series of family rafting experiences, these trips are designed to bring the whole family together on the river! Our feature trips allow time together in the outdoors, sharing the thrills of the river. Join us on one of our spectacular white water rafting adventures & we will show you the magic the river has to offer!
